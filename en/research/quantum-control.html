--- layout: research-detail title: "Quantum Control Schemes" title_en: "Quantum Control Schemes" icon: "🔬" category_id: "quantum-control" image: "/assets/img/quantum-control.jpg" lang: "en" permalink: /en/research/quantum-control/ --- High-fidelity quantum control schemes are central to reliable quantum computing. QCL researches precise qubit control methods, exploring new approaches to optimize quantum gate operations and state preparation. Our research focuses on: - **High-fidelity quantum gates**: Optimizing single-qubit and two-qubit gate pulse waveform design to improve gate fidelity - **State preparation and measurement**: Developing efficient quantum state initialization and high-fidelity readout schemes - **Noise suppression**: Exploring dynamical decoupling and other noise suppression techniques to extend quantum coherence times

--- layout: research-detail title: "Control Systems & Software" title_en: "Control Systems & Software" icon: "💻" category_id: "control-systems-software" image: "/assets/img/control-systems" lang: "en" permalink: /en/research/control-systems-software/ --- Control systems and software toolchains bridge the gap between quantum algorithms and quantum hardware. QCL develops advanced control systems and supporting software platforms for superconducting quantum processors, tightly integrated with high-performance computing (HPC) infrastructure. Our research focuses on: - **FPGA real-time feedback control**: Developing low-latency FPGA-based real-time feedback systems for fast decision-making in quantum error correction and beyond - **AI-assisted quantum circuit compilation**: Leveraging machine learning to optimize circuit compilation and mapping strategies, improving execution efficiency on superconducting architectures - **HPC-integrated quantum control system**: Deep integration of quantum control systems with HPC clusters, leveraging HPC resources to accelerate quantum circuit simulation, calibration data processing, and control parameter optimization, enabling hybrid classical-quantum computing workflows

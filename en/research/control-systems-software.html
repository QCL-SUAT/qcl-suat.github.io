--- layout: research-detail title: "Control Systems & Software" title_en: "Control Systems & Software" icon: "💻" category_id: "control-systems-software" image: "/assets/img/control-systems.jpg" lang: "en" permalink: /en/research/control-systems-software/ --- Control systems and software toolchains bridge the gap between quantum algorithms and quantum hardware. QCL develops advanced qubit control systems and supporting software platforms. Our research focuses on: - **FPGA real-time feedback control**: Developing low-latency FPGA-based real-time feedback systems for quantum error correction - **Multiplexed qubit readout**: Frequency-division multiplexed readout schemes for parallel multi-qubit measurement - **Quantum circuit compilation**: Efficient circuit compilation and optimization methods for superconducting architectures - **Automated calibration framework**: Automated calibration and tuning tools for multi-qubit systems - **Experiment management platform**: Integrated experiment control, data acquisition, and analysis systems

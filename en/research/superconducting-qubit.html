--- layout: research-detail title: "Superconducting Quantum Devices" title_en: "Superconducting Quantum Devices" icon: "⚛️" category_id: "superconducting-qubit" image: "/assets/img/research-bg" lang: "en" permalink: /en/research/superconducting-qubit/ --- Superconducting qubits are one of the most promising approaches to quantum computing. QCL is dedicated to designing and optimizing high-coherence superconducting qubits, exploring novel architectures and fabrication processes. Our research focuses on: - **Transmon qubit optimization**: Improving gate fidelity through design refinement and various couplers, enabling long-range gate operations - **Novel quantum devices**: Exploring artificial giant atoms, waveguide electrodynamics, and other novel superconducting quantum device architectures to expand quantum information processing capabilities - **Fabrication processes**: Improving qubit coherence time through advanced fabrication processes and material selection, developing Josephson junction fabrication methods and substrate processing techniques

--- layout: research-detail title: "Superconducting Quantum Devices" title_en: "Superconducting Quantum Devices" icon: "⚛️" category_id: "superconducting-qubit" image: "/assets/img/research-bg.jpg" lang: "en" permalink: /en/research/superconducting-qubit/ --- Superconducting qubits are one of the most promising approaches to quantum computing. QCL is dedicated to designing and optimizing high-coherence superconducting qubits, exploring novel architectures and fabrication processes. Our research focuses on: - **Transmon qubit optimization**: Improving coherence time and gate fidelity through advanced fabrication processes and material selection - **Fluxonium qubits**: Exploring novel fluxonium architectures that leverage large anharmonicity for higher-fidelity quantum gates - **Fabrication processes**: Developing Josephson junction fabrication methods and substrate processing techniques

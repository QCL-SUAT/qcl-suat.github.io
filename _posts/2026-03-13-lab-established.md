The Superconducting Quantum Computing Laboratory (QCL) was officially established on March 13, 2026 at the Shenzhen University of Advanced Technology. The lab is affiliated with the Quantum Technology Research Center, School of Computing and Microelectronics, focusing on fundamental research and key technology development in superconducting quantum computing.

## Research Directions

The lab has four main research directions:

- Superconducting qubit design and optimization
- Quantum control system development
- Quantum computing software toolchain development
- Superconducting chip micro-nano fabrication

We welcome students passionate about quantum computing to join us.

超导量子计算实验室（QCL）于 2026 年 3 月 13 日在深圳理工大学正式成立。实验室隶属于量子技术研究中心、算力微电子学院，聚焦超导量子计算的基础研究与关键技术开发。

## 研究方向

实验室设有四个主要研究方向：

- 超导量子比特设计与优化
- 量子测控系统开发
- 量子计算软件工具链开发
- 超导芯片微纳加工

## 团队

欢迎有志于量子计算研究的同学加入。
